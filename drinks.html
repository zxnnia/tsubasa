The Irish Cowboy

Shitty irish coffee that just uses cowboy coffee, making it smoother.

  • 50 mL Irish Whiskey
  • 120 mL cowboy coffee
  • 50 mL fresh cream
  • 1 tsp of sugar
    •
  • Make it exactly like you would irish coffee.
  • Dissolve whiskey and sugar into cowboy coffee
  • Top with cream by pouring it on the back of a spoon held above the surface of the liquid.
  • Enjoy
